Services

We build reliability into your operation by combining quality products with expert support, flexible fulfilment, and rigorous compliance.

Strategic Sourcing & Procurement

We maintain relationships with a wide network of manufacturers and distributors to secure genuine, compliant parts at competitive prices. Our team verifies specifications, certifications, and origin so you receive exactly what your maintenance teams expect—no surprises, no delays.

Whether you need a once-off item or a recurring supply contract, we negotiate lead times, manage back-orders, and provide proactive updates so planning stays accurate and downtime stays minimal.

Inventory & Consignment Solutions

For fast-moving or critical spares, we offer tailored inventory programs—from min/max stocking to on-site consignment. This ensures availability for high-priority components without tying up unnecessary capital. Our data-led approach keeps the right items on hand, in the right quantities, at the right time.

We track consumption, forecast demand, and align procurement cycles with your maintenance schedules, improving equipment uptime and budget control.

Technical Matching & Specification Support

Choosing the correct part matters. Our specialists assist with cross-referencing OEM numbers, verifying compatibility, and recommending suitable alternatives where appropriate. We provide datasheets, material safety information, and installation guidance to support your engineering and safety teams.

Expedited Logistics & Delivery

We coordinate end-to-end logistics—including regional couriers and dedicated same-day deliveries—to get parts to site safely and on schedule. From packaging suited to harsh conditions to real-time shipment updates, we manage the details so your teams can focus on critical operations.

Compliance, Quality & Safety Assurance

We align with relevant standards and regulations impacting industrial and mining supply in South Africa. Product traceability, batch control, and documentation are part of our standard process. We can provide inspection reports, certificates of conformity, and MSDS on request.

After-Sales Support

Our commitment continues after delivery. We actively follow up on performance, handle warranty claims with manufacturers, and incorporate your feedback into future sourcing to continuously improve reliability and total cost of ownership.
